Sustainability

As sustainability is a prominent prong in our philosophy;
we ethnically source all our food and produce from local farmers.
Each Farmer is a business partner, a supplier, and most of all a friend.
We also believe in no food waste; all excess food is
re-purposed and put through the biological
process of composting.
